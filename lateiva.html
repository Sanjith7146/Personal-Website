Introducing Lateiva

Lateiva allows you to create events and activity sets for tasks you need to complete before leaving. For example, when going to a party you can select your event, choose the relevant activity set and follow the guidelines on how long to spend on each task. It’s like having a personalised schedule coach in your pocket.

Features

  • Create, edit and delete events with name, location and time
  • Build and customise activity sets for your routines
  • Set durations and priorities for each activity
  • Receive auto‑generated guidelines on how long to spend per activity

My Role in the Project

Lateiva was created by Tanish, Mashal, Sanjay and myself during the 2021 Swift Accelerator Programme. I served as both Chief Executive Officer and Chief Design Officer. As CEO I delegated tasks, organised meetings and ensured our project stayed on track. As CDO I designed the user interface, crafted the app’s visual identity and learnt to utilise SwiftUI effectively. Through this journey I honed my collaboration, leadership and research skills.
